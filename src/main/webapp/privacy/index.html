Although legal requirements may vary from country to country, we intend to adhere to the principles set forth in this Online Privacy Policy even if the your information is collected from the countries that may not require an "adequate" level of protection for your Personal Information.

If you have any comments or questions regarding our Privacy Policy, you may please contact us!
